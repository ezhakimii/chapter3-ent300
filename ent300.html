ENT300 Chapter 3 Interactive Notes

1. What is Entrepreneurship?
Entrepreneurship is based on creativity and innovation, where everything starts with an idea.

Creativity generates unique ideas and innovation turns them into reality.
2. What is Creativity?
- Ability to generate new ideas
- Use imagination to create solutions
- Combine existing ideas in new ways
3. Creative Process
Preparation – gather knowledge
Incubation – ideas develop subconsciously
Illumination – idea appears
Verification – evaluate and implement
4. What is Innovation?
Implementation of creative ideas
Adds value to ideas

Forms:
- Products
- Services
- Processes
5. Types of Innovation
Radical:
- Major changes
- New markets
- High risk, high return

Incremental:
- Small improvements
- Low risk
- Continuous development
6. Forms of Innovation
- Product
- Process
- Organizational
- Management
- Marketing
- Service
7. Sources of Innovation
- Process needs
- Unexpected occurrences
- Incongruities
- Market changes
- Demographic changes
- Perception changes
- New knowledge
8. Relationship
Creativity → generates ideas
Innovation → applies ideas
Entrepreneur → implements ideas
Entrepreneurship → process of using both
9. Importance
- Helps business survive and grow
- Increases profit
- Encourages improvement
- Important for long-term success
